Blocking Updates

You can easily block all future updates on your Nintendo Switch. Here's how!
If you want to delete existing updates already downloaded, go into recovery mode.

Instructions

  1. Turn on your Switch and go to the Settings application
  2. Go to Network settings and scroll down to DNS settings
  3. Change DNS to Manual and change your Primary DNS to 173.255.238.217
    • Leave the Secondary DNS as it was previously

Updates should now be blocked on your system!
