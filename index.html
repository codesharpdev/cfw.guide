Get Started

What is HENkaku?

Homebrew are games and other software which are not officially approved. For example, VitaDoom is a port of the classic game DOOM and mGBA lets you play Game Boy Advance ROMs. You can find more examples here.

What is HENkaku?

HENkaku is the first ever homebrew enabler for PS Vita and PSTV. It is akin to jailbreaking your iPhone or rooting your Android device.
Running homebrew on your Vita is 100% free using HENkaku. No cost, no ads, and no "donations". As long as you're on version 3.60 or below, you're good to go! If you paid for this software, you should ask for your money back.

What is HENkaku Ensō?

HENkaku Ensō enables HENkaku to be installed automatically on boot. HENkaku requires you to reinstall it upon every reboot, however Ensō fixes this.

What can I do with HENkaku?

  • Customize your HOME Menu with user-created themes
  • Backup, edit, and restore saves for your games games
  • Play games for older systems with various emulators, using RetroArch or other standalone emulators.
  • Install homebrew titles to your system, and have them appear on your HOME Menu
  • Dump your game cards to a format you can install, and play them without needing the card
  • Use an SD Card instead of Sony's expensive propriety memory cards
  • Use a USB memory stick or a USB hard drive instead of Sony's memory cards (PSTV only)

    • What do I need to know before starting?

  • Before beginning the guide, you must know the risks of Ensō: EVERY time you modify your system, there is always the potential for an UNRECOVERABLE brick. They're rare, but still a possibility. Installing Ensō may make your device PERMANENTLY UNUSABLE.
  • It most likely won't happen though.
  • This guide will work on both Vita 1000 and Vita 2000 in all regions on firmware 3.60 or below.
  • If everything goes according to plan, you will lose no data and end up with everything that you started with (games, saves, etc will be preserved).
  • Keep your device plugged in and charged throughout the entire process to avoid data loss or damage from an unexpected power-off!
  • You must have a memory card if you are using a Vita 1000 (Fat Version) (OLED Display)
