Get Started

What's FWTool?

FWTool is a DSi Homebrew Application which we will use to dump our Console ID, EMMC CID and NAND

Woah, hold up, what's do all those words mean?

Your Console ID and EMMC CID are going to be used for decrypting and re-encrypting various files throughout the guide. They're essentially the keys to your DSi.
Your NAND is your DSi's internal storage. With your Console ID and EMMC CID, we can decrypt and edit this to our heart's content. This includes installing DSiWare and downgrading System Titles.

Downgrading

Downgrade to any firmware version

Get Started

Why would you want to downgrade?

Downgrading is actually pretty useful. I mean, for starters, to even use RocketLauncher and HiyaCFW, you need to be on 1.4. Remember, it only works on 1.4, so 1.4 is a pretty good firmware to be on.
Furthermore, if you want, you can downgrade certain titles such as the DS Card Whitelist on their own, so you can finally use those flashcards which were blocked from a system update.
