fwTool

We will use fwTool to dump your NAND, ConsoleID and EMMC CID.
If you follow the guide precisely, you will not brick.

Instructions

  1. Download and install a Hex Editor of your choice
    • I recommend HxD
  2. Download DSi SRL Extractor
  3. Download fwTool
  4. Copy fwTool.nds to the root of your DSi's SD card and rename it to boot.nds
    • If you already have a boot.nds, rename it to something else beforehand
    • If your exploit uses a different file name than boot.nds, rename it to that
  5. Put the SD card into your DSi and turn it on
  6. Go to the System Settings Application and then go to Data Management
  7. Copy a DSiWare title to the SD card
    • Make sure it's the only one on your SD Card
  8. Exit out of System Settings and launch your exploit game into fwTool
  9. Choose Dump CID and Dump BIOS
    • These BIOS files are required for testing your NAND in No$GBA
  10. Finally, choose Dump nand_dsi.bin
    • This will take a few minutes
  11. Choose Exit and your DSi will power off
  12. Take out your SD Card and put it in your computer
  13. Extract DSi_SLR_Extractor.zip to somewhere on your computer
  14. Go to the SD:private/ds/title directory
  15. Copy the .bin file to the DSi_SLR_Extractor folder
  16. Drag the .bin file
  17. Open the .footer file in your Hex Editor

You're now good to launch the Homebrew Launcher and install Haxchi!
