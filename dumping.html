Game Dumping

Dump your physical (or digital) games into digital format. No need to carry around your games anymore!
Make sure you have sufficient space on your memory card/microSD card

  • Click this link on your Vita to download MaiDumpTool
  • Open VitaShell and navigate to "ux0:download"
  • Select "MaiDumpTool_V233.2zEx_eng.vpk" and install it
  • Open MaiDumpTool from the Home Menu
  • Remember that while using MaiDumpTool, the O button confirms, and the X button cancels, similarly to Japanese models
  • Select "Extract/Decrypt Content"
  • Select the game you wish to dump
  • Select "Extract game and decrypt eboot"
  • Your game has now been dumped!
