Updating to 5.5.1 (Game)

There's no stable browser exploit for 5.5.2, so we must update your system with a game to 5.5.1.

You can update to 5.5.1 with a copy of:

  • Cars 3: Driven to Win
  • Darksiders: Warmastered Edition
  • Just Dance 2017
  • Minecraft: Story Mode - The Complete Adventure
  • Paper Mario Color Splash
  • Shantae: Half-Genie Hero
  • Skylanders: Imaginators
  • The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

Instructions

  1. Disable your Wi-Fi
  2. Launch your disc
  3. Update your system to 5.5.1

You're now good to launch the Homebrew Launcher and install Mocha!
