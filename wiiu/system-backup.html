System Backup

In case anything goes wrong, you'll want to back up your system NAND before installing CBHC. While optional, we heavily recommend that you do.

Note that if you have a 8GB/32GB Wii U, you'll need around 8GB/32GB of free space on your SD card respectively

Instructions

  1. Download the latest release of Wii U NAND Dumper
  2. Extract the contents of the Wii U NAND Dumper .zip to the root of your SD card
  3. Insert the SD card into your Wii U
  4. Launch the Haxchi title while holding A to launch the Homebrew Launcher
  5. Launch the Wii U Nand Dumper
  6. Change all of the options on screen to Yes
  7. Press A to begin dumping
    • The process may take up to 3 and a half hours for 32GB consoles
    • The process may take up to 1 hour for 8GB consoles
