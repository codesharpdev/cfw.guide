Updating to 5.5.2

Haxchi can only be installed on versions 5.5.0 to 5.5.2, so we must first update your system.
I really shouldn't need to be showing you how to do this, but I'll put it here anyway.

Instructions

  1. Power on your Wii U and open the System Settings
  2. Press the R button a few times to get to System Update
  3. Update your system

You're now good to launch the Homebrew Launcher and install Haxchi!
