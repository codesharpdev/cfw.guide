Different firmware versions will require different steps to achieve the end goal of sysNAND CBHC CFW. This page will help you find where to start for your firmware version.
If you do not wish to pay the extra money, you can opt for using Mocha CFW on 5.5.1, however you'll have to load up CFW on boot every time.

Updating to 5.5.1 via game requires one of the following games:

  • Cars 3: Driven to Win
  • Darksiders: Warmastered Edition
  • Just Dance 2017
  • Minecraft: Story Mode - The Complete Adventure
  • Paper Mario Color Splash
  • Shantae: Half-Genie Hero
  • Skylanders: Imaginators
  • The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

Click the firmware of your device to take you to the appropriate page. To check your version, go to the Settings application. Your version should be displayed on the gamepad.
