Dumping Info

Dumping info is recommended but optional. We dump this info so we can recover our vWiis at a later date in the unlikely event that anything goes wrong.

Preparations

  • You must use an SD Card with around 2GB of free space.
    • Now the vWii is hacked, we can use SDHC/SDXC SD Cards with a capacity higher than 2GB
  • Download XYZZY
  • Update your system

Instructions

  1. Power on your Wii U and open the System Settings
  2. Press the R button a few times to get to System Update
  3. Update your system

Proceed to Homebrew Channel
