Tales of Symphonia: Dawn of the New World - Eri HaKawai

This step will help you install Homebrew using Tales of Symphonia: Dawn of the New World.

Preparations

  1. Download Eri HaKawai
  2. Download Hackmii Installer
  3. Extract the private folder from the Eri HaKawai .zip to the root of your SD Card
  4. Extract the boot.elf file from the Hackmii Installer .zip to the root of your SD Card
  5. Turn on your Wii U and go to the vWii Menu
  6. After it has loaded, put in your SD Card
  7. Go to Wii Options > Data Management > Save Data > Wii
  8. Go to SD Card and select the Tales of Symphonia savegame
  9. Copy the savegame to the vWii

Instructions

  1. Boot your Tales of Symphonia disc
  2. Start tbe game and load the savegame your just copied over
  3. Press the + button to enter the game menu
  4. Scroll to the STATUS button and press A
  5. Scroll to the monster named Eri HaKawai and press A
  6. Hackmii Installer should now load

Installing the Homebrew Channel

  1. You will be informed that you should not have paid for this software
    • We didn't, so that's good
  2. After a few seconds, you will be able to proceed
  3. Install the Homebrew Channel
    • Not BootMii, you can't install that on a vWii
