LEGO Batman - Bathaxx

This step will help you install Homebrew using LEGO Batman.

Preparations

  1. Download Bathaxx
  2. Download Hackmii Installer
  3. Extract the private folder from the Bathaxx .zip to the root of your SD Card
  4. Extract the boot.elf file from the Hackmii Installer .zip to the root of your SD Card
  5. Turn on your Wii U and go to the vWii Menu
  6. After it has loaded, put in your SD Card
  7. Go to Wii Options > Data Management > Save Data > Wii
  8. Go to SD Card and select the Bathaxx savegame which corresponds to your game region
  9. Copy the savegame to the vWii

Instructions

  1. Boot your LEGO Batman disc
  2. Load the saved game you just copied
  3. You will now be in the Batcave
  4. Take the elevator on the right side
  5. In the Trophy Room, go to the upper left corner and go through to door
  6. You will now enter Wayne Manor
  7. Now you can select a character
  8. Choose the last enabled one in the lowest row
  9. Hackmii Installer should now load

Installing the Homebrew Channel

  1. You will be informed that you should not have paid for this software
    • We didn't, so that's good
  2. After a few seconds, you will be able to proceed
  3. Install the Homebrew Channel
    • Not BootMii, you can't install that on a vWii
