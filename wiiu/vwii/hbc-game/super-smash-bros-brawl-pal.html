Super Smash Bros. Brawl - Smash Stack

This step will help you install Homebrew using LEGO Batman.

Preparations

  1. Turn on your Wii U and go to the vWii Menu
  2. Put your SD Card in and boot your Super Smash Bros. Brawl disc
  3. Go to the Stage Builder
  4. Move all custom stages to the SD Card, including pre-packaged ones which come with Super Smash Bros. Brawl
  5. Leave the stage builder and take your SD Card out of the Wii U
  6. Insert the SD Card into your computer
    • Do not turn off your Wii U or exit Super Smash Bros. Brawl
  7. Rename the private directory to private.old
  8. Turn on your Wii U and go to the vWii Menu
  9. Download Smash Stack
  10. Download Hackmii Installer
  11. Extract the Smashstack_PAL/private folder from the Smash Stack .zip to the root of your SD Card
  12. Extract the boot.elf file from the Hackmii Installer .zip to the root of your SD Card

Instructions

  1. Insert your SD Card again
    • If you turned your Wii U off or exited Super Smash Bros. Brawl like I told you not to, redo all previous steps
  2. Enter the Stage Builder
  3. Hackmii Installer should now load

Installing the Homebrew Channel

  1. You will be informed that you should not have paid for this software
    • We didn't, so that's good
  2. After a few seconds, you will be able to proceed
  3. Install the Homebrew Channel
    • Not BootMii, you can't install that on a vWii
