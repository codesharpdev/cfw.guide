Installing CBHC

Coldboot Haxchi (CBHC) is basically just Haxchi on boot.
You don't need to go and click on the Haxchi title to launch you CFW, it does this all automatically.

You MUST have a working Haxchi installation!

Your Haxchi title MUST be on the Wii U's System Memory

You MUST have bought your Haxchi title from the eShop

You MUST remove all USB devices before proceeding

Failing to follow these instructions will RESULT IN A BRICK

In the unlikely event that anything goes wrong, cfw.guide, its owner, and FIX94 will not be held responsible for any damage done to your console.

Preparations

  1. Download the latest release of CBHC
  2. Extract the contents of the CBHC ".zip" to the root of your Wii U's SD Card
  3. Put the SD Card into your Wii U

Instructions

  1. Remove all USB devices from your Wii U
    • If you haven't done this yet, there's a chance that you didn't read the massive red boxes above.
    • Read them or you will brick
  2. Enter the Homebrew Launcher by holding A while launching your Haxchi DS Virtual Console Title
  3. Launch the CBHC installer
  4. Ensure that:
    • You have Haxchi installed
    • Your Haxchi installation is functional
    • You bought your Haxchi title from the eShop
    • Your Haxchi title is on the System Memory
    • You have removed all USB devices
  5. Read the warning that appears
  6. Press A to install
  7. Once it's done, you'll be kicked back to the Home Menu
  8. You may now reconnect any USB devices which were disconnected earlier
  9. Reboot your Wii U
  10. Go down to "Autoboot: Disabled"
  11. Press A to enable autoboot
  12. Press A on "Boot System Menu"
  13. Reboot again to make sure everything works
  14. Move your CBHC install to somewhere where it won't annoy you

NEVER uninstall the CBHC DS Virtual Console Title

NEVER reinstall the game you installed CBHC on from the eShop

NEVER move the CBHC DS Virtual Console Title to a USB Drive

NEVER delete the user account NNID that purchased the game you installed Haxchi on

NEVER format your Wii U while CBHC is installed

Doing any of the above will PERMANENTLY BRICK your Wii U

Proceed to Homebrew Launcher (Channel)

Press Home while CBHC is starting to access the CBHC boot options menu.
From here, you'll be able to:
  • Boot System Menu > Boots sysNAND
  • Boot Homebrew Launcher > Boots the Homebrew Launcher from sysNAND
  • Boot Mocha CFW > Boots Mocha CFW from redNAND (Not needed)
  • Boot fw.img on SD Card > Boots a fw.img based CFW from SD Card (Not needed)
  • Boot vWii System Menu > Boots into the vWii Menu
  • Boot vWii Homebrew Channel > Boots into the Homebrew Channel for vWii

    • Proceed to Homebrew Launcher (Channel)
