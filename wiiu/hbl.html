WiiU Guide

Homebrew Launcher

This step will enable you to launch user-made custom content on your Wii U, which has not been signed or authorised by Nintendo.

Preparations

  1. Download the Homebrew Launcher
  2. Download the Homebrew App Store
  3. Copy the contents of "homebrew_launcher.v1.4.zip" to the root of your Wii U's SD Card
  4. Copy the contents of "appstore15.zip" to the "SD:wiiu" directory on your SD card
  5. Put the SD Card into your Wii U

Instructions

  1. Power on your Wii U and open the Internet Browser
  2. Navigate to cfw.guide/go
    • Alternatively go to loadiine.ovh to launch the exploit
  3. That's it!

You are now able to download whatever homebrew you want with the Homebrew App Store, and launch it by going to cfw.guide/go

Proceed to Installing Haxchi

Note that if you are on 5.5.2, it is heavily advised to install Haxchi, as the browser exploit on 5.5.2 is very unstable.
