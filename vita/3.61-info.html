3.61 - 3.65 HENkaku Info

Currently there is NO public HENkaku exploit available for PS Vita Firmwares 3.61 and above.
The most you can do is install PSP Homebrew and ISOs on versions 3.63 and 3.61.

On firmwares above 3.60:

  • You cannot use HENkaku
  • There is no software way to downgrade
  • There is no exploit for these firmwares being worked on
  • If anyone says they are, they are lying
  • There are no plans to create an exploit for these firmwares
  • HENkaku cannot be ported to these firmwares
  • That website you were going to tell me about is fake
  • There are only two ways to downgrade, both requiring hardware replacements.

    • These ways are:

  • Selling your old Vita and buying another
  • Replacing the motherboard with one which is on 3.60 or below.

    • Here are all the possible hacks available on firmwares newer than 3.60:

  • Running PSP Homebrew/ISOs (3.63 and below)
  • Changing PSN accounts
  • Custom Themes
  • Decrypt and modify CMA backups

    • All New PS Vita Systems will be on 3.60 or lower!

    With the exception of the Red & Silver Japanese models
