USBMC

Use an USB memory stick instead of those expensive memory cards

This guide is for PSTV consoles only.
PS Vita handheld consoles are not supported, as they do not have a USB port.

For this guide, you will need:

  • A Vita on FW 3.60
  • usbmc_installer.vpk

Instructions

  1. Install usbmc_installer.vpk onto your PSTV
  2. Once that's done, open the USBMC Installer app from the Home Menu
  3. Press X to install the drivers and reboot
  4. Enter the USBMC Installer app again
  5. Press X to copy the files to your USB

Now, whenever HENkaku is launched, the USB will automatically mount.
If you have HENkaku Enso installed, it will automatically mount on boot.
