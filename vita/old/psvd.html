PSVD

This guide requires you to have already installed your PSVD board.

This guide is for PS Vita 1000 (Phat) 3G consoles only.
PS Vita 2000 (Slim), PS Vita 1000 (Phat) Wi-Fi and PSTV consoles are not supported.

For this guide, you will need:

  • A PS Vita 1000 (Phat) 3G on FW 3.60
  • A PSVD board already installed
  • usbmc_installer.vpk

Instructions

  1. Install usbmc_installer.vpk onto your PS Vita
  2. Once that's done, open the USBMC Installer app from the Home Menu
  3. Press X to install the drivers and reboot
  4. Enter the USBMC Installer app again
  5. Press X to copy the files to your SD Card

Now, whenever HENkaku is launched, the SD card will automatically mount.
If you have HENkaku Enso installed, it will automatically mount on boot.
