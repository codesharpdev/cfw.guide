Supercard DSONEi/DSONEi Mini NTRBoot? - 23/08/17

On GitHub, dark-samus stated that "out of all the DS One related stuff lately, this card's updater is actually the least complex." They also mention that if they had more info on the card, and some better quality pictures, they might be able to support this card in the future.
So, everyone who owns a DS ONEi/DS ONEi Mini, you might be in luck! if you want to help out, send some pictures

DSTT NTRBoot! - 21/08/17

It seems that NTRBoot for the DSTT is on the way. Currently, this installation requires a lot of manual work, and will be released when a proper installer is ready.
