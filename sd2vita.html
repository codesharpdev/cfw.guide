Vita Guide

SD2Vita

Use an Micro SD card instead of those expensive memory cards

For this guide, you will need a:

  • A Windows Computer (Vista and above)
  • A Vita on FW 3.60
  • An FTP Client (WinSCP/Filezilla should do the trick!)
  • Win32 Disk Imager
  • SD2Vita Drivers
  • zzBlank.img file

    • Instructions

  • Back up your PS Vita memory card
  • Move your "ux0:tai" folder to "ur0:tai"
  • Add the following to "ur0:tai/config.txt"
    *KERNEL
    ur0:tai/gamesd.skprx
  • Put the microSD card into your computer
  • Open Win32 Disk Imager and select zzBlank.img from your computer
  • Select your microSD and click "Write"
  • Take out your microSD card and put it back in again
  • Format your disk from Windows
  • File System: exFAT
  • Allocation Unit Size: Default Allocation Size
  • Do not put a volume lable
  • Copy your backup to the SD Card
  • Put the SD Card into the SD2Vita adapter and reboot your Vita

    • Help

    Q: My SD2Vita adapter is stuck in my Vita! How do I get it out?
    A: Use a toothpick or another thin object to gently pry it out.

    Q: My SD2Vita adapter keeps popping out of my Vita! How do I get it in?
    A: Apply a small bit of tape to the side of the adapter. This will give it the extra thickness it needs to stay in.
