Vita Brick Safety

Keep your Vita from blowing up

  • DO NOT perform file operations inside the os0 partition. This may brick your device!
  • Ensure that safe mode is turned on (accessable from "Settings > Henkaku Settings") when you are using your device.
  • You need safe mode disabled for this guide though, make sure to keep it enabled while following it.
  • Be careful when installing VPKs. Past dumps (such as Fruit Ninja and Lego Harry Potter) have had bricking applications injected into them, bricking your device
